Formulas & Grading
Semester Performance Index
SPI = Σ (Grade Pointsi × Creditsi) Σ Creditsi (in the semester)
Cumulative Grade Point Average
CGPA = Total Grade Points (all semesters) Total Credits (all semesters)
Equivalent Percentage
% = ( CGPA − 0.5 ) × 10
Subject Grade Points (G)
G = (Theory Grade Pts × Theory Credits)
    + (Practical Grade Pts × Practical Credits)
Overall Subject Marks = Weighted Sum Formula
